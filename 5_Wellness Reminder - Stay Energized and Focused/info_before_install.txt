Introduction:
Welcome to Stay Energized and Focused! This Python program helps you stay focused and energized by providing reminders for water intake, eye exercises, and physical exercises at user-defined intervals. It aims to promote a healthy lifestyle and productivity by encouraging regular breaks and hydration.

Features:
- Provides reminders for water intake, eye exercises, and physical exercises.
- Customizable interval settings for reminders.
- Audio cues for reminder notifications.
- Log of completed actions with timestamps.
